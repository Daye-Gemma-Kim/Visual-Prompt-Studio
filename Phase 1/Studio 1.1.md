Assignment title: Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

Prompt: Create a hyper-realistic digital mural and LED billboard advertisement displayed on the largest screen at the center of Times Square, surrounded by skyscrapers and glowing city lights at night.This artwork functions as a form of social protest and public awareness campaign that inspires discussion about animal adoption and ethical responsibility. Its main meaning is to criticize the commercialization of pets and encourage compassion through adoption and donation.
The billboard is divided vertically into two contrasting sections in a representational, photorealistic style.
Left side: A dark, gloomy, and depressing atmosphere. A crying puppy trapped inside a glass pet shop display, surrounded by people holding money and observing it like a product. Cold lighting, muted gray and blue tones. In the center of the left section, bold text reads: “Dogs Feel Pain Too.”
Right side: A bright, warm, and hopeful atmosphere. A happy adopted dog playing with a smiling person in a peaceful outdoor setting. Soft sunlight, vivid colors, warm green and yellow tones. In the center of the right section, text reads: “Adopt for Happiness.”
The strong visual contrast symbolizes the tension between consumerism and compassion. Cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed textures, emotional storytelling, high resolution, professional advertising design, social justice theme, animal adoption advocacy, 8K quality.
